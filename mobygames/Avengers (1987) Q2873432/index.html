Description

Avengers is a 2D top-down beat'em up. The main villain, "Geshita", has captured 6 girls, and the player's character must defeat him and his men. The gameplay is very similar to many other beat'em ups, like Double Dragon but the action is presented in a different way - the player eliminates the dozens of enemies with fists and legs (he has 3 types of attack - punch, kick and roundhouse). He also collects the various power-ups - bonuses improve his strength, speed, or give additional weapons.
