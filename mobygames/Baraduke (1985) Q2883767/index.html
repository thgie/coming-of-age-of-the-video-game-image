Description official descriptions

Baraduke is an action shooter game where the player takes control of a spacewoman (player 1 is Kissy and player 2 is Takky) moving around in a low-gravity environment with the help of a jet pack, as they investigate the once-peaceful Planet Paccet, which has been taken over by aliens known as Octy.

The game consists of eight stages, each stage is split up into six floors, with five regular floors and a boss floor. In the first four floors in each stage, the player must clear the floor of Octy, then they can exit the level through a chute located at the outside walls of the level. In the second last floor, there are no Octy but lots of capsules, which can hold one of four things: a yellow coin object which gives the player 100 points, a ray gun that increases gun level by one, a Paccet, which can earn you extra shield in the end-of-floor bonus games or the Bagan, which is a Pac-Man-like creature that will attack the player.

Trivia

Character names

Player 1 character "Kissy" is named after the game's programmer Yoshihiro Kishimoto, while player 2 character "Takky" is named after the designer of this game Yukio Takahashi.

Music

Music from this game appears on Namco Video Game Graffiti and Namco Game Music Vol.2 albums.

Protagonist

According to Namco’s official canon, the protagonist of Baraduke - Masuyo Tobi (nicknamed "Kissy") was once married with Taizo Hori from Dig Dug and they had three sons: Susumu Hori, Ataru Hori and Taiyo Tobi (from the Mr. Driller series).
