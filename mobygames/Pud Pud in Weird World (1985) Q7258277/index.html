Description official descriptions

You as 'Pud Pud', a Pac Man like being with razor teeth, little legs and wings instead of ears, have the task of escaping 'Weird World'. Finding all 10 bowls of pudding scattered around the world will allow you to escape it. You either walk around the platforms or flap your wings to fly in the air to navigate the world's mazes.

You have to manage your energy which is constantly getting lower by eating certain creatures and avoiding others. Beware not to meet Mrs. Pud, her kiss is deadly! Reducing the energy supply to zero will result in the loss of one of three lives.
