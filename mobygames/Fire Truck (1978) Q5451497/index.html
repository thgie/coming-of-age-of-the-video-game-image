Description

Fire Truck is a cooperative driving game in which two players control a fire truck. The game's hardware is based on Super Bug. Two players can play or one of the two roles can be taken over by the AI. The game was also released as Smokey Joe, where the internals of the game are identical but the cabinet doesn't have the second player's station so the player is forced to play with the AI. There is no actual fire to fight, the only thing the players fight is control of the truck which must be driven as far as possible until fuel runs out.
