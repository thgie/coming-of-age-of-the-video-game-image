Description

In Sheriff you control a sheriff whose girlfriend has been kidnapped by a gang of bandits. The gang has surrounded you, and you must shoot them all while surviving the storm of bullets coming at you. The more bandits you shoot, the faster the rest get. You are enclosed in a box in the center surrounded by dots and four barriers. These protect you, but they also block your shots. Each dot takes one bullet to destroy, but it's possible to shoot between them. Occasionally bandits will enter from the corners and circle you.
