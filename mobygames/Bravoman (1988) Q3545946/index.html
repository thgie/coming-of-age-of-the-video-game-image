Description official description

The evil Dr. Bomb has taken over Nitta village and only you, an ordinary salary man, can stop his evil plans by transforming into the fabulous superhero Bravoman!

The game is a side-scrolling action platformer. As Bravoman, you have the power to elongate your members depending on how long you press each button allowing you to jump higher or punch enemies on the other side of the screen.

Features 22 stages filled with bizarre settings and enemies, all rendered using colorful 2D graphics.
