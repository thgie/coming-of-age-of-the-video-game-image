Official Description (Ad Blurb)

Hopper

for the BBC Microcomputer Model B

Hop the frog across the busy motorway trying to avoid four lanes of fast-moving traffic. To get across the river to the frog's lair you must leap on to the logs and turtles' backs, but beware of the diving turtles, the crocodile and the snake.

Complete with music and full sound effects, dragonfly, timer and table of high scores.

Acornsoft Hopper is fully compatible with either keyboard or joysticks.

Source: Back of Cardboard Folder - BBC Micro (UK)
