Description official descriptions

One of the first edutainment games for NES.

Donkey Kong gives Junior some math problems ranging from simple to more challenging. Move up and down the vines to find the correct answers as fast as possible.

Trivia

Character names

Donkey Kong Jr. Math is a two-player simultaneous title, with Donkey Kong Jr. as Player 1, and a pink ape who represents the second player. The second player ape, however, has never been revealed to have an official name.

Edutainment

Donkey Kong Jr. Math is the only title officially released under Nintendo's "Edutainment" banner.
