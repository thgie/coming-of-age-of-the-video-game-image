Description

The arcade game is back! Destroy those aliens before they destroy you. With three lives, you must fight back at the aliens with your space ship as they come closer and closer. Can you wipe them out before they wipe you out?

A screen full of aliens coming closer and closer is not the easiest situation to be in. As the aliens get closer, their speed will increase, making it very difficult to stop them. Good hunting!
