Description official description

Khalaan is a strategy game of empire building set in the ancient Arabic world. Turn based, it requires you to establish trade, raise armies, and manage your economy to build a strong empire. You can expand your empire through conquest and diplomacy. Turns are seasonal, and military, naval, and trade caravans are the tools of empire.
