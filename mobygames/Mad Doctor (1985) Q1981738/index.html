Description

In this obscure little game you play as a mad doctor and your goal is to build your very own monster. To achieve this, you have get a hold of a number of dead bodies using all means necessary like digging up graves or killing innocent villagers. These bodies are then taken to your lab where you can dismember them and put them back together using various medical instruments.
