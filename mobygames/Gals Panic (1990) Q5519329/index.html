Description

Gals Panic is a Qix-like game where you guide a cursor over an area and avoid various enemies. As a new twist, you get rewarded by seeing a women in various states of dress (and undress), depending on your score. The image is initially covered by an overlay, which you have to wear away, at the same time avoiding and defeating enemies. Most of the overlay is translucent, but it is opaque over the girl. Your goal is to uncover as close to 100% of the picture as possible. Each round, she will wear less clothes. After she's topless, you move on to another girl.
