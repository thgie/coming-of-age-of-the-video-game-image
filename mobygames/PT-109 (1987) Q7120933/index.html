Description

PT-109 is a classic torpedo boat simulation, set in the World War II. It is a game with great depth of options, realism, accuracy of simulation and enriched with an outstanding documentation.

45 different missions, 5 levels of play, practice tactics mode, 4 theaters of action (Philippine islands, Solomon islands, New Guinea and Mediterranean sea) and many other options and features.
