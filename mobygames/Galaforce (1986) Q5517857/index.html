Description

Galaforce is a straight forward shoot-em-up. Your ships encounters wave after wave of alien ships which attack you in formation. Some ships require more than one hit to destroy them. Every 10th wave or so there is a super wave consisting of hordes of ships which require multiple hits to destroy.
