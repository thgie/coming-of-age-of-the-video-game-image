Description official description

It's the year 2020 and the Earth is under control of the evil druglord Pit Bull and his multitude of brainwashed servants. You play as Shadow, an elite narc on a mission to save the future. Raid 2020 is a side scrolling action game for one player. Using the different weapons that can be found, you need to fight off the many servants of Pit Bull and avoid exploding mines, lasers, and other obstacles throughout different locations before taking on Pit Bull himself to become victorious.
