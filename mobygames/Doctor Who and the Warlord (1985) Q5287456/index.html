Description

This second licensed game based on the Doctor Who sci-fi television series is a text adventure.

In the first part, you are looking for the Doctor on a strange planet where you encounter androids, interstellar gypsies and King Varangar's moody blood guards. You must obtain information through conversations and exploring areas by typing various text commands. Objects must also be collected in order to complete the game.

In the second part, you travel in the Tardis back in time to the Battle Of Waterloo where you must defeat both Napoleon and the evil Warlord.

There are 250 different locations in each part.

Trivia

Reissue

This game was reissued as "The Warlord" - in this version all the Doctor Who references are removed. The Doctor himself is renamed Tim Trevyl, and the Tardis becomes the Cubix.
