Description

Ziriax is a side-scrolling futuristic "shoot-'em-up" set in a futuristic environment, similar to R-Type. The player controls a spaceship-like aircraft that is trying to survive amidst the fire of hostile ships and other enemies. There are five stages in the game altogether; each contains a fight against a boss enemy at the end. Small spheres are dropped by enemies; collecting these spheres bestows the player-controlled ship with various power-ups. These include two additional small ships above and below the main one, an ability to shoot backwards, and missiles that can be shot diagonally.

Trivia

Back cover

On the back cover, the ad blurb says:

(...) 90% Amiga Joker - these are just some of the accolades Ziriax has received so far."

The magazine did not test Ziriax until it was already out in retail and gave it 48%.
