Description official description

This is the third of Ultimate Play the Game's Sir Arthur Pendragon series of games. You find yourself on a ghost ship. You are searching for the captain's soul. You must avoid the obstacles in the game and find the necessary items needed before opening the captain's tomb. The player can use various weapons to defend themself from enemy skeletons. They can also collect items of jewellery, which increase the score but have no other function.
