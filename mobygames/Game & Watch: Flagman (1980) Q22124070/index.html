Description

Game & Watch: Flagman, also known as Flag Man (two words) in the USA in the Time Out series by MEGO, is the first of the Game & Watch models to offer two games in one, which didn't just differ from each other by speeding up the game for an advanced difficulty. The second G&W game to appear in the so-called Silver Series.

In game A, the player has to use his or her memory by repeating a sequence of numbers appearing at the on-screen character; numbers 1 and 2 are on flags behind his back and numbers 3 and 4 are on both his feet. Each number has its corresponding sound/volume pitch. Game B is a game of reflexes where the player has to push the right button asked by the onscreen character as quick as possible.
