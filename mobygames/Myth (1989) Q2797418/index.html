Description official description

Magnetic Scrolls' last adventure before the introduction of their 'Magnetic Windows' format, Myth is classic interactive fiction.

Myth is set in Ancient Greece, place of myth and legend - hence the title. You are are Poseidon, the Sea God. You have to journey to the Underworld to get the Dark Helmet of Hades.

Its basically a tiny treasure hunt game where you get to play Poker with Charon and Death.

It is a very short game with a cunning puzzle or two.

Trivia

Release

You could only obtain Myth via the British Adventure club, Official Secrets, a subdivision of the mail order games retailer Special Reserve. It was not available for retail sale. Also, each Myth disk was personalized with the owner's name and Official Secrets membership number.
