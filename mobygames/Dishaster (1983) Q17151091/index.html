Description

You've seen it at circuses but can you keep those plates spinning on top of those skinny sticks?

In Dishaster, you have a plate(s) spinning on top of a skinny stick. After a time, the plate begins to wobble. When this happens, move under the plate and press your button, repeatedly, to get the plate up to speed again. If a plate falls, if you can get to it before it hits the ground, it will be placed back on the stick. Your score is based on how long you keep plates spinning. After four plates fall and crash, the game is over.
