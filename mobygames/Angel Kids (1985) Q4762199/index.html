Description

You play the role of two Angel Kids as you attempt to get a girl from the ground up into the sky to a cloud over various stages. The way to get the girl skywards is to bounce her off a rope and you move one angel to the left and one to the right to make the rope taut to bounce the girl even higher. As you move upwards there are objects that when hit, give you bonuses but if the objects are hit at the wrong angle then the girl bounces around the screen. On the right of the screen is a distance gauge showing you the distance between the ground and the cloud, as well as your position. If you let the girl go past the angels then you lose one of three lives and you have to start the stage again.
