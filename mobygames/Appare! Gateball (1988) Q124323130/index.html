Description

Appare! Gateball is a sports game featuring gateball, an Asian sport similar to croquet. The game consists of two teams of five players each. One team plays with a white ball, and one team plays with a red ball. The red team starts first. The goal is to pass the ball through three gates and hit the peg. Included options are playing on either a grass field or sand field, and choosing between 12 players, each with their own stats.
