Description official description

Ball is a DSiWare game based on the Game & Watch LCD toy of the same name. You play as a juggler who is juggling balls. The DSiWare version mimics the LCD look with the "ghost" images that you can see in addition to the lit up images. There are two different modes of play - in GAME A, you juggle two balls, while in GAME B, you alternate between three. Each time you catch a ball, your score increases by one point. When you drop a ball, the game ends.
