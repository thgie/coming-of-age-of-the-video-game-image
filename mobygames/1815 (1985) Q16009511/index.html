Description

1815 is a strategic war game. It takes place during the Napoleonic Wars in 1815. The display of the game is a 2D view from above. There's a green field with obstacles like trees and rivers. There are 2 opponents: the greens and the blacks. Each opponent starts with 25 units from 6 different types. The goal is to destroy all the opponent's units.
