Description official description

Based on the graphic novel of the same name, this computer game Quest for the Time-Bird is a marvellous saga whose principal ingredients are action, danger, suspense, fantasy and magic.

You have nine days to complete your quest by exploring this vast unknown world of wizards and spells with Roxanna, daughter of Mara the witch and Bragon, a retired knight who once again goes back into a life of danger, all for the great Quest of the Time-Bird!

Trivia

Disks

This was the first Atari ST game to only be available on double-sided disks, meaning that owners of very early STs would need a new external drive to play it.
