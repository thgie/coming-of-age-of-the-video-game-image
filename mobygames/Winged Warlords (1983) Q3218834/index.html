Description

Winged Warlords is 2D single screen action game, literally a clone of Joust, but now this is survival on one level. The player controls character riding a flying warhorse and the objective is to defeat the other riders. If enemy riders fall down, the player can pick them up for points. Sometimes a indestructable red bird, the "Evil Bird of War" flies onto the battle arena and tries to kill the player's character. The player has 4 lives, and a live is when hit by enemies. When all lives are lost, the game is over.
