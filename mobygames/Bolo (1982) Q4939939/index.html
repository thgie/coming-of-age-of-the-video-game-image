Description

The objective of Bolo is to maneuver your tank through the maze, seek with your radar and destroy 6 enemy bases. Each base is continuously manufacturing a variety of opponent tanks. The tank types vary from the primitive drone version encountered early in the game to the faster torpedo tanks and the deadly hunter/killer tanks. The intelligent assassin tanks will pursue you at high speed by the most efficient route.

Trivia

Credits

Bolo never had the name of the game designer/programmer Jim Lane anywhere in the game or on the packaging because Lane worked at Microsoft at the time.

License

This game was created based on a license of the sci-fi classic series Bolo: Annals of the Dinochrome Brigade by Keith Laumer. The game Ogre was also based on this license.
