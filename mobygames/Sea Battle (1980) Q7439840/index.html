Description official descriptions

Sea Battle is a strategic game for two players only. Each player controls a fleet of ships including Submarines, Destroyers, and Battleships. The goal is to engage and sink enemy ships. The winner is the player who commands the troop transport ship into the opponent's harbor. There are two phases in Sea Battle; the strategy phase, and the combat phase. The strategy phase includes maneuvering your fleet into different strategic positions on the map, laying mines, and repairing fleets. Opponents engage in ship-to-ship warfare in the combat phase, triggered by the ENGAGE button. Each of the different types of ships have their own strengths and weaknesses, such as weapon range and maneuverability.

Trivia

Release history

The Atari 2600 version of this game was completed by APh Technological Consulting for Mattel in 1982 but not released. Mattel had created a new rule that all games had to be playable by one player, at least.This game was shown at the Classic Gaming Expo in 1999 and the response was favorable enough that Intellivision Productions sold it at the Classic Gaming Expo in 2000.
