Description official descriptions

You are a heroic pirate. Your lady love has been kidnapped! You must brave obstacles, enemy men, and beasts to save her!

The game consists of four areas. In the first area, you must swing on ropes from masts. Then, in the second area, you must swim shark-infested ocean waters. Next, you must run up a mountain jumping or ducking boulders. Last, you must get past two enemy pirates to rescue your lady. After this, the areas repeat but at a harder difficulty level.
