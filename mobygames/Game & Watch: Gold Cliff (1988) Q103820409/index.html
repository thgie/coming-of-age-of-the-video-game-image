Description

Game & Watch Multi Screen: Gold Cliff is the fourteenth game in the fourth (and biggest) series of Game & Watch games published by Nintendo, also known as the Multi Screen Series, consisting of 15 titles. Unreleased in Japan.

You play as an archaeologist on his way to many expeditions. In order to reach the treasures, you'll need to avoid the enemies as well as precisely jump on the many disappearing platforms, whilst not forgetting the key to unlock the door on the upper levels. After a few levels the explorer faces the guardian of the temple which must be defeated to progress further. Gold Cliff is composed of 24 worlds and 144 different levels and is the first G&W game to feature a "continue" option.
