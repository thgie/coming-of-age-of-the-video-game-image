Description official descriptions

In 2087, a physicist, inventor of a time travel machine, assembles a group of Guardians to travel back in time and thwart the assassination of President Kennedy. Try to convince everyone that his life is in danger and have an actual face to face meeting with Kennedy.

Guardians of Infinity: To Save Kennedy is a text adventure with a few graphical images. The player inputs verb commands, communicating with the agents and instructing them to perform various tasks to prevent the assassination. There is a time limit imposed on the game. The gameplay heavily relies on the usage of the manual, since it contains vital information concerning the whereabouts and schedules of key people that the player must interact with in order to accomplish the goal.

Trivia

Manual

Packaged with the game is a manual detailing the whereabouts of everyone connected with the Kennedy assassination in the weeks leading up to the shooting. It also serves as off-disk copy protection: Looking up the info is directly related to the gameplay, and you can't win the game without it, because you won't know where to send your agents to meet a particular person at a particular time.
