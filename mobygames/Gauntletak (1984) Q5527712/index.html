Official Description (Ad Blurb)

THE THRILLS AND EXCITEMENT OF THE TOP ARCADE GAME IN THE PALM OF YOUR HAND!

NINE LEVELS OF DIFFICULTY!

**This action game contains nine levels of fun and excitement. Test you wits against the evil demons and grunts that are blocking your warrior from accomplishing his quest. Your weapons include a secret key, an axe, or a bomb, but watch your power level...once you lose all your power you lose your chance to discover the sacred orb...and to win the game.

Features include sound ON/OFF, highest level retention and automatic power off timer.**

(screenshot captions)

Level 1 - "The Castle"

Use the key to open the Door of Bricks and then kill the monster.

Level 2 - "Dark Forest"

You are in great danger! Swarms of monsters are after you, but if you use the bomb you can kill them all at once.

Level 3 - "The Lost Caverns"

Determine which bricks are movable and the maze will suddenly change.

Level 4 - "The Unseen"

An extremely difficult maze since it is hard to find your way out as the walls are invisible.

Source: Back of Package (US)
