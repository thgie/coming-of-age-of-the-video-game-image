Description

Game & Watch Wide Screen: Octopus is the second game in the third series of Game & Watch games published by Nintendo, also known as the Wide Screen Series.

In this game, one has to guide three explorers equipped with old-fashioned scuba gear pass a waiting octopus to get a hold of the sacks of gold hidden in a shipwreck. The player has to get as much gold as he can without getting touched by the giant squid. If the latter touches the character he's immediately captured by it. Game B is not much different only an increase of speed and therefore the difficulty.
