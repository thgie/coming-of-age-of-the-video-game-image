Description official descriptions

Penguin Wars is the sequel to Penguin-Kun Wars. It is a dodgeball game played with animals. There are ten balls for each player, with the goal being to hit the opponent the most amount of times to win the match and advance up the leader board.

Characters can also be evolved through playing Story Mode. The characters then can be played in versus mode with up to four human opponents, both locally and online. The screen can be viewed both horizontally and vertically. Music from the original 1985 game has been remixed and updated, and new tracks are available as well.
