Description

Gun Fight is a two player twitch game in which players take on the role of a gunfighter in the American West. Four way joysticks allow the player to move the gunfighter in different directions on the screen. A two-way analogue stick allows players to aim the gun up and down. Bullets ricochet off the top and bottom of the screen. For each hit players earn points and more destructible obstacles are added on the screen. After a time limit is reached the player with the most points wins.
