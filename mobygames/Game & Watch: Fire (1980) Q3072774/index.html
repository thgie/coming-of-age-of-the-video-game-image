Description

Game & Watch: Fire, also known as Fireman Fireman in the USA in the Time Out series by MEGO, is the fourth game in the Silver Series and the first G&W game to introduce the option to save your best score. This score can be displayed by pressing the Game A or Game B button, however it will be lost on a battery change or system reset.

The concept of the game is to guide the medics with their stretcher who have to catch people falling from different floors of a building on fire. In Game A the player needs to time the bouncing of the victims on the stretcher just right, like a trampoline, until they fall directly into the ambulance. In Game B, the goal stays the same, but the player has to deal with 9 bouncing people at the time.
