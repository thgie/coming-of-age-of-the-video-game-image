Description official descriptions

Starbrain, a giant super computer programmed for destruction, is threatening the Galactic Empire. As a skillful and experienced star soldier, you need to pilot the Caesar into the space station Starbrain has occupied. The space station is heavily guarded by enemy ships and strange robotic creatures. You will be able to find different weapons along the way to power up the Caesar and increase chances of a successful mission. Star Soldier is an arcade style vertically scrolling shooter for one player.

Ready to intergalactic challenge?

The Good
Star Soldier had good graphics, good sound and nice gameplay. It featured power-ups, like back-shooting, and a very good artificial intelligence. It was developed by Hudson Soft, the company which also developed the classic Bomberman.

The Bad
Star Soldier was a bit harder, even in the first level.

The Bottom Line
If you has an NES or japanese GBA, get it and enter the intergalactic adventure! An NES Hudson Soft's classic! Recommended.

NES · by Gustavo Henrique dos Santos (97) · 2014
