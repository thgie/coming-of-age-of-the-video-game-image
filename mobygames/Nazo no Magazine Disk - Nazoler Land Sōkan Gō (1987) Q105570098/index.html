Description

This Famicom disk contains eight different mini-games. The mini-games are:

  • Kaiten Meiro Doorman - Players have to traverse several floors of a maze. However, freely moving around the maze is made difficult because of gates that can only swing open certain ways.

  • Nazoler Uranai - A Mastermind variant featuring signs of the zodiac. Figuring out the correct answer will reward the player with a fortune telling message.

  • Super Jigsaw - As the name suggests, a jigsaw puzzle game. Players can only switch one tile with another one at a time.

  • Momoko-hime o Sukue! - A first-person adventure game. Players explore a dungeon labyrinth on a mission to rescue Princess Momoko.

  • Nihon Isshū Ultra Quiz Ryokō - A quiz game. If the player answers a question right they will be awarded money which they can then use to travel to different sites in Japan.

  • Famicom Tekiseikensa - Players take the role of a cowboy in a duel against another cowboy. Whoever can press the fire button the most times in ten seconds wins the duel. This game is for one or two players.

  • Oshidashi Puzzle - Players have to shoot various rows and columns of tiles into their correct position. When a tile is in the correct position it changes from it's original color to another color. This game features three different levels.

  • 3-D Maze - A first-person maze game.
