Description official descriptions

The player drives a racing car up to a maximum speed of 218 km/h across a highway course. In each course, the player must dodge other cars, avoid going off the road and make jumps on ramps, while trying to utilize the available fuel. After making a large jump across a gap, the player finishes the course and leftover fuel grants bonus points. The player then proceeds to the next course. There are four courses. When all the courses are complete, the player loops to the first course. The game ends when the fuel runs out.
