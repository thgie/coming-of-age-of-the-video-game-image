Description

You are the Wummel in the city of the Spectars. Your fight for justice is not done.

Spectar is a sequel to the game Targ and you must drive your battle cruiser around a grid. Drive over any twinkling stars you can and shoot any of the enemy cruisers you come across. A level ends when you have shot all the enemies or collected all the stars.
