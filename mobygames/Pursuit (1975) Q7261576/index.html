Description

In Pursuit you fly a World War I biplane and attempt to gun down enemy aircraft. A flight stick is used to steer the plane and to get the enemy in view. When the enemy plane is in your crosshairs, press the top-mounted fire button to shoot them down and gain points. The game features Atari and Kee's Durastress technology and the crosshair is provided via an overlay. This was the first game to be released after Atari publicly revealed that Kee Games was actually a subsidiary rather than a competitor.
