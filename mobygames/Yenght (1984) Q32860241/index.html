Description

This adventure consists in crossing the mysterious world of Yenght, trying to find the Source of Youth, indispensable condition for completing the quest successfully. The game begins with a complicated labyrinth, in which the key to the exit (and eventually the exit itself) must be found. This is no easy task, as the game offers no maps, and enemies are lurking in the dark corners of the labyrinth. Once outside, the real adventure begins. Many locations of the game are maze-like, the challenge often lying in finding the correct way through the game's environments.
