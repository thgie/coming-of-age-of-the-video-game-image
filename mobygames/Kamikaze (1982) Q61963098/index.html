Description

Kamikaze is 1-player World War II themed arcade game for the Apple II.

The player controls a small military vessel, which is under attack by an unending barrage of Japanese military aircraft. The ship can move horizontally, and has the ability to fire vertically upwards to destroy oncoming enemy planes. Red aircraft worth 50 points continuously stream from the left, while low flying green aircraft worth 25 points come from the right. The player must avoid bombing runs from the enemy aircraft, while attempting to maximize enemy bombers destroyed. When the player is hit, a life is lost. 5 lives lost, and it's game over.
