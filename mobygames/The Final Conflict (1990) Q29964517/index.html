Description official descriptions

A Risk-type game of global conquest, only with nuclear weapons and real-time action. Click on various countries and invade to (hopefully!) expand your empire. Setups include the standard Cold War variant and random starting countries.

Average score: 2.5 out of 5 (based on 9 ratings with 1 reviews)

Missile Command meets Risk, and they have a mutant baby.

The Good
The graphics and sound were ok. That's about it.

The Bad
This is Risk, only real-time and with nukes. Managing a global empire at accelerated speed? Yeah, it's every bit as unwieldy as it sounds. Final Conflict is utterly unrealistic and not much fun either, so I don't know why anyone would spend time playing it.

The Bottom Line
Please, don't try this at home--or anywhere else!
