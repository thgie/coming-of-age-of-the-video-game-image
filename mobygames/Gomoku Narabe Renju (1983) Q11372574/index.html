Description

In Gomoku Narabe Renju, players take turns attempting to connect 5 of their pieces in a straight or diagonal line without being blocked by their opponent. Each individual game won gives the player a point. The player with the most amount of points after a set number of games wins the overall game. The game offers three difficulty modes. On the hardest difficulty, the player has to win the game before a time counter counts down to zero.
