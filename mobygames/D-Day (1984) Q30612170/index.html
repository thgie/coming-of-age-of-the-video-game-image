Description official description

D-Day is a computerised two player war game written in BASIC, representing World War II's historical invasion into Normandy. It contains four different scenarios, loaded separately detailing different phases of the invasion (Landing, Breakout, Road to Arnheim and Arnheim Landing). The players take turns as the Allies and Germany first by deploying their troops and then to manoeuvre them towards victory. To to win a scenario, a player must defeat the opposing side.
