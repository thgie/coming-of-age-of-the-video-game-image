Description official description

Monty Mole was imprisoned due to his coal theft in Wanted: Monty Mole, and the aim of the player as the mysterious masked mustelid, Sam Stoat is to rescue Monty from prison.

The game is a multi-screen side-view 3D perspective maze game where the objective is to get a key from the governor's office to let Monty free. There are eight doors in all and Monty can be behind any of them. Sam must return to the office after opening each door to get a new key. Potions of invincibility and guns that hold 5 bullets can be picked up along the way to assist in the mission. Once Monty is rescued he has to be taken out of the prison and "off into the sunset".
