Description official description

A baker tries to bake and deliver bread while fighting raccoons. Pieces of bread are moving along a factory line. The raccoons try to eat your bread and also try to switch off your machines. You have to keep your machinery running and also scare away the raccoons. If you do that your delivery truck will be loaded with bread and drive off.

Trivia

Cancelled Spectrum release

A Spectrum version was advertised (alongside many other Imagine/Konami tie-ins), but never released.

Music

A remixed version of the song is included in the first stage's music in both the NES and Game Boy versions of Ocean's Jurassic Park game and in the first two stages in the Game Boy version of Jurassic Park 2: The Chaos Continues. Both were composed by Jonathan Dunn.
