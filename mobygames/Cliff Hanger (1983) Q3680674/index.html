Description

Cliff is on a mission to save Clarissa from being forced to marry the evil Count Draco. Cliff Hanger is an interactive cartoon movie. The game consists of animated scenes, during which the player has to press direction buttons or the sword button in the right moment to trigger the next segment of the movie. The anime video used in the game are scenes from the Lupin III anime movies, mainly scenes from The Castle of Cagliostro and the Mystery of Mamo animated movies. Cliff in the game is Arsène Lupin III in the original anime.
