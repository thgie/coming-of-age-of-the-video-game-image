Description

Quest Adventure is a traditional text adventure with a RPG spin, where the titular quest is to find an ancient scroll hidden in a monster infested land.

You can choose between taking the role of either a wizard, cleric, rogue, fighter or a simpleton, each with their own strength and weaknesses. In addition to the text based interface you can partake in RPG like combat with the various monsters you encounter along the way.

The interface is text driven with split screen graphics.
