Description

Odyssey: The Compleat Apventure is a turn-based role-playing game. It consists of multiple parts and places the player in the role of the leader of an army who sets out to defeat an evil wizard. The game contains elements of Dungeons & Dragons and combines elements of two earlier games written by the same author: Dungeon Campaign and Wilderness Campaign.

The game features three major sections. Except for the endgame section, the gameplay is presented in a top-down map with minimalistic animation and sound effects.

Ambitious Early Apple II Adventure

The Good
This game has a lot of stuff going for it. It touches a few genres such as adventure, simulation, and role playing. Once you learn the basics of the gameplay, it can be addictive.

The Bad
Being an early Apple II title, some of the sounds can be grating. There are a few bugs that will crash the game, or cause some strange artifacting. The sailing phase of this game can be a little boring and frustrating.

The Bottom Line
A must see game that really was ahead of it's time and nothing really like it since.
