Description

Bloody Afternoon is a horror-themed arcade shooter controlled with the mouse. Skeletons and other monsters have taken over a spooky castle, and they have a number of hostages. It is your job to shoot your way through a series of static scenes where enemies and hostages will appear in various places on the screen, popping up over walls and through windows. You will sometimes need to shoot at the right point on the targets, for example aiming for the head if an enemy is wearing chest armor, or aiming at a hostage's arm restraints to free them without harming them. There are three difficulty settings.
