Description

Basketball is a 1 - 2 player basketball game for the TRS-80.

A 1 on 1 basketball game, where each basket is worth 2 points. Each game is 5 minutes in length, and there are 5 levels of computer difficulty. Two players can also compete for basketball dominance. Players can take the ball from their opponent, and can take a shot on the basket when close.
