Official Description (Ad Blurb)

A CHALLENGE FOR ALL AGES

Two exciting games in one game cartridge that strengthen your math skills while you have fun!

MATH MASTER

You're a gorilla out for a stroll along the banks of the river. Suddenly, your path is blocked by an elephant! How to get around? Why, by solving a math problem, of course! Get it right and you're on your way. Wrong, and it's into the river with you! Think or swim!

  • Set the difficulty level from 1 to 18!
  • Play by yourself or against a friend!
  • Two players can compete at different difficulty levels to even the odds!

FACTOR FUN

Now here's a challenge! We give you a bunch of numbers and an answer. You supply the math – addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division – to get that answer from those numbers! How close can you come?

  • On-screen adding machine shows your progress!
  • Battle the clock or a friend!
  • 7 difficulty levels!

MATH MASTER is an enhanced version of the previously released MATH FUN. FACTOR FUN is an entirely new game!

Source: Back of Box - Intellivision (US)
