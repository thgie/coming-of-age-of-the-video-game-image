Description official descriptions

A unique puzzle game in which you play a fish like creature who must explore the many play fields collecting gold bars that form a picture. Enemies litter the field, which you can blast away with a wave of energy, removing them for a short amount of time from the game. You also need to grab onto the posts and walls to turn into the gaps on the field, adding an element of timing to the game.
