Description

Face Off is an ice hockey game. It allows up to four players to play simultaneously. It features a one team each from the following countries: USSR, Canada, Sweden, Czech Republic, USA, Finland, France, and Japan. There are two modes. The first is Tournament mode, in which one or two players face off against four computer teams. In Versus mode, three or four players, with two players on each team, play against each other. The player(s) choose how many players are on the team at the start. After each period, the player is required to add another credit.
