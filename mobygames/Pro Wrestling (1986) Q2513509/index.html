Description official description

Pro Wrestling is an action wrestling game for one or two players. By using different controller combinations you can perform a wide variety of moves to try and beat your opponent in a 5 minute match. In the one player version, you need to fight your way to first place (but the game will be over if you fall below 6th place). There are seven different wrestlers to fight before you can be declared the Video Wrestling Association champion!

Trivia

Title screen music

Some of Nintendo's early NES sports titles used the same tune during the title screen. Some of the games featuring this opening tune are Pro Wrestling, Tennis, Volleyball, and Baseball.
