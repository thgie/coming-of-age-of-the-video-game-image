Description

You, as Guy Fawkes are on a mission to blow up the Houses of Parliament. In order to do so, you must guide Guy through the vaults and the crypts collecting sticks of dynamite and fireworks. To accomplish your mission, take a special firework to the start screen and light it on fire.

The game is a standard platform game with ladders and elevators where you must avoid the denizens of the catacombs.
