Description official descriptions

California Games II is the sequel to the smash hit 1988 game.

This time you compete in: hang-gliding, jetski, skateboarding, body boarding and snow boarding.

Trivia

Body Boarding

If more than one player is competing on the Body-Boarding event, one of them is in for a bad surprise: once the character jumps off the deck, he'll be eaten by a shark (just like the Jaws movie poster). Of course he doesn't die, it's just a prank!
