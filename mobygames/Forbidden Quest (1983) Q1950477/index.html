Description official description

Your one man spaceship crash lands on a barren planet. You must take it upon yourself to look for remnants of any type of civilization on the planet in 103 locations in this text adventure game.

Trivia

Extras

The game actually came with five 9.5 x 11.5 sized cards for clues. One was colored and the other four were black and white. All the cards were on hard back cardboard like paper and had a sheen type gloss on the front.
