Description

Galactic Warriors is a 2D robot-themed versus fighting where you take control of one of three selectable robots and fight other robots from around the solar system.

The fighters to choose from are Samson (equipped with a laser sword), Gaea (shooting projectiles) and Poseidon (can extend his arms). You battle the four-legged projectile shooting Urunes on Mars. The second opponent, Giger is armed with a whip. You fight him on an ice landscape. Third is Boreas who fight with a chain weapon with Saturn on the background. Fourth is Asura, a robot with wings and laser eyes. Fifth is Orion, a large robots who fights with his hands on a space station. The sixth and last battle is on earth against the character you have chosen. Each fighter has their own fighting techniques witch include kick, blocks and weapon attacks. You can play solo or against a friend.
