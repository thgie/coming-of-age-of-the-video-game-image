Description

In Jet Fighter, two planes fly around with the goal of shooting the other as many times as possible in a dogfight within the time limit. When a plane is hit, an explosion occurs, and the plane recovers after a few seconds. As with many Atari games of the era, if the plane flies off the side of the screen, it comes back on the opposite side.
