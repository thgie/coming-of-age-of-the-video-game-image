Description

While transporting a load of precious cargo for your homeworld of Earth, hostile aliens cause your ship to crash land on the planet of Oo-Topos and take you captive. Your goal is to bust out of prison, reclaim the ship's cargo, and perform the impromptu ship's repairs necessary to get off the alien planet.
