Description

Space War, also released as Space Laser and Intruder is a fixed screen shooting game in a science fiction setting. The game is played with two players at opposing sides of a cocktail table. Both control a tank at the bottom of the screen (from their perspective) and can fire a laser with the aim to destroy each other. This is made difficult by a large amount of spaceships flying horizontally past and blocking the shots unintentionally. An upright cabinet version also exists, here the player can only play against the AI.
