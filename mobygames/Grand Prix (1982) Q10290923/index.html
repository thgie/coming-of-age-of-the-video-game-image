Description official descriptions

Grand Prix is a singleplayer action racing game. The game is played with an overhead view of the racetrack which scrolls horizontally. The goal is to reach the finish line in the shortest possible time on one of the four different tracks included. Of course, there will be obstacles on the tracks which will slow the player's car down if they're not careful. Numerous other cars will often get in the way, oil slicks will cause the player's car to temporarily lose control, and on the more difficult tracks, narrow bridges will cause the player's car to come to a complete stop if they miss the path.

Trivia

Activision patch

Players who beat the qualifying times for any race could send in a photo of their TV screen as proof and Activision would send them an Activision Grand Prix Driving Team patch. To get the patch, you had to beat the Watkins Glen race in 35 seconds or less, the Brands Hatch race in 1:00 minute or less, the Le Mans race in 1 minute 30 seconds or less, or the Monaco race in 2:00 minutes or less.
