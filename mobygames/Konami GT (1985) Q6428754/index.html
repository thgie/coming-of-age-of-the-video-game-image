Description

Konami GT is an arcade racing game, a little similar to Out Run, but with a 1st person perspective. The player drives a red sports car which must reach checkpoints without running out of fuel - to do this, he must collect gas icons. Various cars and motorcycles are on the road - the player must avoid them. The car has "turbo" mode - which increases the car's speed, but drains fuel quickly.
