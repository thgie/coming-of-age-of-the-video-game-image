Description

This computer coloring book is another part in the Electric Crayon series and it has 30 pictures of different world places, such as Kremlin, Redwood, Taj Mahal, Fuji, Alps, etc., with Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles to color.

Pictures could be colored from a palette of 16 colors. Choosing a color and clicking in the picture do a color fill in the area clicked. The finished, or in progress, coloring was automatically saved to disk. Player may also remove all colors applied previously to start coloring from the very beginning.

Each picture has an educative description and could be printed.
