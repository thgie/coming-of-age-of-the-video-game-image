Description official descriptions

In this game, you are trying to search for the evil skull of souls. You begin on a small island where the evil skull has summoned many minions to eliminate you. You discover that many people have tried to obtain this skull. Their graves are inside the cave, and their life force may help you or try to kill you. The ultimate forces of chaos and darkness are gathering as they meet you in the cave. The aim of the game is to destroy the skull of souls. As Sir Arthur Pendragon the player can use various objects to progress through the game; a spade can be used to dig holes, a magical energy cloak grants temporary invulnerability to many enemies. Enemies can also be dispatched by shooting magical orbs at them.
