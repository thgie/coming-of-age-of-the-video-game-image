Description

Game & Watch Micro Vs. System: Donkey Kong 3 is the second of three released games in the eight series of Game & Watch games published by Nintendo, also known as the Micro Vs. System Series. This series allowed for two players to battle against one another on the same screen. The Micro Vs. series boasted a wider screen and the innovative storage feature of the two small joy-pads and their wires inside the shell of the system.

Donkey Kong 3 sees the returns of Stanley, the young gardener, who killed insects in the G&W game Green House in what is essentially the direct sequel to that game. Stanley (on the right side of the screen) has to face Donkey Kong (on the left side of the screen) by using insecticide to force parasites on the opposite way. When the insects reach one of the two character's garden, then points are scored by the opponent end a "miss" is given to the unlucky one. The amount of gas loaded in once's device is limited, so when the gauge reaches empty, you'll have to refuel your spray with the drops coming from the corners of the screen. In order to balance the game it's possible to increase the rate of falling drops to refuel your insecticide. Game A sees you battling against a CPU, whilst Game B sees you battling a human player.
