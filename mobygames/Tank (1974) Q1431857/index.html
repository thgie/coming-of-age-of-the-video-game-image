Description

Tank is a two-player game. Each player controls a tank, and their goal is to destroy each other. However, this will be difficult, as they are located in a block maze on top of a minefield. Mines are each shown as an "X." The players move toward each other using two joysticks each, and shoot each other with a fire button on top of their right joysticks.
