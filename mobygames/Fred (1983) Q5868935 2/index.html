Description

Sir Fred is a platform game in which the titular character must infiltrate the great castle of Beni-Gómez in order to rescue his beloved princess. The player guides Sir Fred through the different rooms of the castle until he completes his quest. The protagonist must avoid many hazards on his way, and use various objects that would help him to reach his destination.
