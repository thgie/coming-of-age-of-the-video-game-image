Description official descriptions

Big Top allows you to take on the role of Wendell, a rookie acrobat who must retrieve the ringmaster's hats, and stop the chaos that resulted because of their disappearance. In your way are clowns that will hinder your progress, armed with the likes of knives and beach balls.
