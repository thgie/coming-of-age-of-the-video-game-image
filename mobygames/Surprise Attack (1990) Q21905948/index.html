Description

Surprise Attack is 2D platform game, little similar to Rolling Thunder or Shinobi. The player must neutralize terrorists, who take base on mars, and defuse time bombs. Gameplay is quite simple - hero goes right, shoots enemies, finds bombs, avoid enemy bullets etc, he must jump on upper floors to bypass obstacles (and neutralize some bombs)
